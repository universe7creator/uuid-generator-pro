Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between UUID v1 and v4?

UUID v1 is timestamp-based and includes the time of creation, making it sortable chronologically. UUID v4 is completely random and offers better privacy since it doesn't expose any system information.

Are the generated UUIDs truly unique?

UUID v4 provides 2^122 possible combinations, making the probability of collision astronomically low (approximately 1 in 2.71 quintillion). For all practical purposes, they are unique.
